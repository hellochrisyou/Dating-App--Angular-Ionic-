This messaging app is a practical example of how to integrate ionic framework in action, and a demonstration of proper code use.

What is this app for?

This app is designed to demonstrate my abilities to use Ionic and Angular's in conjunction together. I used Firestore (Firebase's database) to store my NoSQL data.

Why do you use NoSQL?

NoSQL offers leniency with database manipulation. Instead of being restricted with key constraints and relationships, NoSQL allows you to create your tables without a schema-based tables.
